A Global Problem

The Failure of DIY Solutions

Improvisations compromise hardware and fail due to lack of technical specification:

  • Resin and Epoxy Domes: Excessive thickness causes distortion and prevents charging.
  • Latex Membranes and Gloves: Opaque materials that blind the sensor and don't last.
  • Tapes and Bandages: Porous, they peel off with sweat and can oxidize the original sensor.
